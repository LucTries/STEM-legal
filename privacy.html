Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

STEM (Secondary Terminal, External Manager) is a personal content management tool developed and used solely by its creator, Luca Ciocan. It is not a public product or service.

Data Collection

STEM does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties. All data processed by STEM — including video files, transcripts, and metadata — remains on the user's local machine.

Platform Integrations

STEM integrates with third-party platforms including YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram solely to upload content on behalf of the authenticated account owner. No data from these platforms is stored beyond what is necessary to complete the upload.

Authentication

OAuth tokens used to authenticate with third-party platforms are stored locally on the user's device and are never transmitted to any external server controlled by STEM.

Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, contact the developer directly.
